About AUAAS

Founded in 2024 — Canada’s first fully remote, student-led UAS design team.

We collaborate across provinces to design, build, and test custom unmanned aerial systems for student competitions.

Our remote-first approach leverages digital twins, simulation, and distributed prototyping to turn distance into an advantage.

  • Interdisciplinary talent from computing, science, business, and more
  • Focus on autonomy, safety, and competition-grade reliability
  • Committed to mentorship, documentation, and inclusive teamwork
