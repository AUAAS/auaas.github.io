Our Team

Canada’s first fully remote, student-led UAS team — united across provinces.

Leadership

  • Team Captain — sets vision, coordinates, represents the team
  • Group Managers — lead sub-teams and track progress

Technical Group

  • Airframe — structure, materials, aerodynamics
  • Avionics — electronics, flight control, comms
  • Payload — sensors, cameras, water systems
  • Computing — autonomy, simulation, algorithms
  • Safety & Compliance — regs, checklists, competition safety

Operations & Pilots

  • Pilots — Advanced RPAS certification; testing & competition flights
  • Logistics — procurement, shipping, distributed equipment
  • Documentation — proposals, reports, presentations

Admin & Communications

  • Treasurer — budgeting, expenses
  • Funding — sponsorships, grants, partnerships
  • Outreach & Media — website, social media, events
